<p><p>Three phase DC AC inverters are used to convert DC power from batteries or solar panels into AC power for use in homes, industries, and other applications. Two main market types for three phase inverters are pure sine wave and modified sine wave inverters. Pure sine wave inverters produce a smooth, high-quality output similar to grid power, making them ideal for sensitive electronics. Modified sine wave inverters produce a choppy output, suitable for basic appliances and tools but may cause issues with sensitive electronics.</p></p>
