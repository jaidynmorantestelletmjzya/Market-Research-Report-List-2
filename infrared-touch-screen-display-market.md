<p><p>Infrared touch screen display market types can be categorized into stand-alone HMI and embedded HMI. Stand-alone HMI refers to touch screen displays that are independent devices used for specific functions like information kiosks or interactive displays. On the other hand, embedded HMI involves touch screen displays that are integrated into larger systems or machines, such as industrial control panels or medical equipment. Both types serve different purposes and cater to different industries based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
