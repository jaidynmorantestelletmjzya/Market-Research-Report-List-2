<p><p>Hot water buffer tanks are essential components in heating systems to improve efficiency and reduce energy consumption. There are two main types of hot water buffer tanks: Diaphragm Buffer Tanks and Air Bag Buffer Tanks. Diaphragm buffer tanks use a flexible diaphragm to separate the water from air, while air bag buffer tanks use a balloon-like bag to achieve the same purpose. Both types help regulate water temperature, reduce cycling of the heating system, and provide a consistent flow of hot water.</p></p>
