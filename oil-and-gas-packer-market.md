<p><ul><li>Permanent Packer</li><li>Retrievable Packer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent packers are designed to stay in place once installed and are commonly used in wells where there is no need for removal or adjustment. Retrievable packers, on the other hand, can be easily removed and reinstalled as needed, making them more versatile and cost-effective in wells that require frequent maintenance or adjustment. Both types of packers play a crucial role in oil and gas operations by isolating different zones within a wellbore to optimize production and ensure safety.</p></p>
