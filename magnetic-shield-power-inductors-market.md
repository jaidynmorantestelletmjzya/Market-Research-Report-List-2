<p><p>Magnetic shield power inductors are used in electronic devices to store and manage energy. The market for these inductors can be categorized into two main types: multilayer power inductors and wirewound power inductors. Multilayer power inductors are constructed with multiple layers of windings, offering higher inductance values and power handling capabilities. Wirewound power inductors, on the other hand, are made with a single wire wound around a core, providing lower inductance values but higher current ratings. Both types play a crucial role in various electronic applications.</p></p>
