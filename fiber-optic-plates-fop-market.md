<p><p>The Fiber Optic Plates (FOP) market consists of two main types: straight and tapered plates. Straight FOPs have a uniform thickness throughout the plate, providing consistent performance across the entire surface. Tapered FOPs, on the other hand, have a thickness that gradually decreases toward the edges, allowing for improved light coupling efficiency and enhanced image resolution. Both types of FOPs have their own unique advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
