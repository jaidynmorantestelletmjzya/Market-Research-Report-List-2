<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household pod coffee machines are available in two main market types: closed source system and open source system. Closed source systems use proprietary coffee pods that are sold by specific brands, limiting consumers to using only those pods. In contrast, open source systems allow for the use of a wider variety of coffee pods from multiple brands, offering more flexibility to consumers. Closed source systems typically have higher quality control and consistency, while open source systems offer more variety and options to choose from.</p></p>
