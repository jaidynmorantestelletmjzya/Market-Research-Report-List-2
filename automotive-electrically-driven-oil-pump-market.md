<p><strong>The Automotive Electrically Driven Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Pump</li><li>Separate Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electrically driven oil pump market consists of two main types: integrated pump and separate pump. Integrated pumps are integrated into the engine and are responsible for circulating oil throughout the engine. Separate pumps are standalone units that can be placed in various locations within the engine bay to perform oil circulation functions. Integrated pumps are more common in modern vehicles, while separate pumps are often used in older or high-performance vehicles for more precise oil flow control.</p></p>
