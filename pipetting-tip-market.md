<p><p>Pipetting tips can be classified into two main market types: non-filtered and filtered. Non-filtered pipette tips are standard tips used for basic liquid handling applications. Filtered pipette tips, on the other hand, have a built-in filter to prevent contamination and aerosol formation during pipetting. These tips are commonly used when working with sensitive samples or when using volatile or hazardous liquids. Both types of tips are essential tools for accurate and precise liquid handling in laboratories.</p></p>
