<p><p>Analog proximity sensors are classified into different types based on their working principle. Inductive proximity sensors detect metal objects, capacitive proximity sensors detect non-metal objects, and supersonic proximity sensors use sound waves to detect objects. Other types of analog proximity sensors include photoelectric sensors and magnetic sensors. Each type of sensor is suitable for different applications depending on the material being detected and the distance at which detection needs to occur.</p></p>
