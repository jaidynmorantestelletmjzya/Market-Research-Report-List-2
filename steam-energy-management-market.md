<p><p>Steam energy management involves various market types to optimize energy efficiency in industrial processes. Control systems help regulate the steam flow to ensure optimal performance. Heat exchange systems transfer heat between steam and fluids, increasing efficiency. Hydrophobic systems prevent water buildup in steam systems, reducing energy loss. Condensing recovery systems recover waste heat from steam for reuse. Waste heat and pressure recovery systems capture and utilize wasted energy. Lastly, there are other markets that offer various solutions to manage steam energy effectively in different industrial settings.</p></p>
