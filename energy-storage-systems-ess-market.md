<p><p>Energy Storage Systems (ESS) market can be divided into several types based on the materials used for energy storage. These include Lithium, Lead Acid, NaS (Sodium Sulfur), and Others. Lithium-based ESS are popular for their high energy density and long cycle life. Lead Acid ESS are cost-effective and widely used in stationary applications. NaS ESS have high operating temperatures and are suited for large-scale energy storage. Other types of ESS include flow batteries, flywheels, and supercapacitors, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
