<p><p>Platform lifts are commonly used in buildings for individuals with mobility challenges. The two main types of platform lifts are vertical platform lifts and inclined platform lifts. Vertical platform lifts move straight up and down, providing accessibility between different levels. Inclined platform lifts travel on an incline along stairs or ramps, making them a convenient option for buildings where a vertical lift is not feasible. Both types of platform lifts are essential in providing equal access and independence for individuals with disabilities.</p></p>
