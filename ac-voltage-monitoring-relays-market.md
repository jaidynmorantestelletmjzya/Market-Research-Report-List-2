<p><p>AC Voltage Monitoring Relays are categorized into Single-Phase and Three-Phase market types based on the number of electrical phases being monitored. Single-Phase relays measure voltage in a single circuit, while Three-Phase relays monitor voltage in three interconnected circuits. Single-Phase relays are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications, while Three-Phase relays are typically employed in industrial settings where three-phase power distribution systems are prevalent. Both types of relays play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and safety of electrical power systems.</p></p>
