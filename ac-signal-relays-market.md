<p><strong>The AC Signal Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TH Mounting</li><li>SMT Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC signal relays are commonly available in two market types - TH mounting and SMT mounting. TH mounting refers to Through-Hole mounting, which involves soldering the components onto the PCB through holes, providing strong mechanical support. On the other hand, SMT mounting stands for Surface Mount Technology, where components are soldered onto the surface of the PCB, allowing for smaller and more compact designs. Both types offer their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
