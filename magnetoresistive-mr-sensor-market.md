<p><p>Magnetoresistive MR sensor market is segmented into various types including Thin Film Magnetoresistive Sensor, Anisotropic Magnetoresistive Sensor, Giant Magnetoresistive Sensors, Tunnel Magnetoresistive Sensors, and others. Thin Film Magnetoresistive Sensor utilizes a thin film of magnetic material to measure changes in resistance. Anisotropic Magnetoresistive Sensor detects changes in resistance based on the direction of magnetic fields. Giant Magnetoresistive Sensors use alternating layers of ferromagnetic and non-magnetic materials. Tunnel Magnetoresistive Sensors rely on the quantum mechanical tunneling effect. Other types include AMR sensors, AMR angle sensors, and more.</p></p>
