<p><strong>The OTP Display Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>OTP Display Card Market includes event-based (HOTP) Tokens and time-based (TOTP) Tokens. Event-based tokens generate a one-time password based on a specific event or transaction, providing enhanced security. Time-based tokens generate passwords periodically, typically every 30 seconds, adding an extra layer of protection. Both types of tokens are commonly used in industries requiring secure access to sensitive information, such as banking, healthcare, and government sectors. Overall, the OTP Display Card Market caters to different security needs by offering a variety of token options.</p></p>
