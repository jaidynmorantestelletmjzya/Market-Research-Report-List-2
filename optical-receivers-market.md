<p><strong>The Optical Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Receivers Market includes three main types: Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (PDH), Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH), and Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (SPDH). PDH is an older technology that allows for different network elements to operate at slightly different clock rates. SDH is a more advanced technology that ensures all network elements are synchronized to a common clock signal. SPDH combines the flexibility of PDH with the synchronization of SDH, providing a balance between the two technologies.</p></p>
