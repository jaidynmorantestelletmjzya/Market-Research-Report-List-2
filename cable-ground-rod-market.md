<p><p>Cable ground rods are an essential component in electrical grounding systems. There are three main types of cable ground rods: double tongue ground rods, single tongue ground rods, and screw compression ground rods. Double tongue ground rods have two connection points for cables, providing extra stability. Single tongue ground rods have one connection point and are simpler in design. Screw compression ground rods have a threaded end that allows for easy installation and secure grounding connections. Each type is suitable for different grounding applications based on their unique features and advantages.</p></p>
