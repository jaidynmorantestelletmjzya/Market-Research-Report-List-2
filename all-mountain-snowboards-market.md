<p><p>All-Mountain snowboards are designed for versatility on various types of terrain. True Twin snowboards have identical shape and flex patterns on both ends, making them ideal for riding switch and performing tricks. Directional Twin snowboards have a symmetrical shape but with a slight directional stance for better control at high speeds. Tapered snowboards have a wider nose and narrower tail, providing better float in powder and smooth turns on groomed runs. Each market type offers different characteristics to cater to the preferences of riders.</p></p>
