<p><p>Meat speciation testing is a process used to determine the specific species of meat in a given food product. There are different types of testing methods used for this purpose, including Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Molecular Diagnostic testing. PCR is a technique that amplifies DNA sequences to identify specific species, while ELISA detects proteins from different species. Molecular Diagnostic testing involves analyzing genetic markers to identify the species of meat present in a sample.</p></p>
