<p><p>Portable Gaussmeters are available in two main types - AC Gaussmeters and DC Gaussmeters. AC Gaussmeters are designed to measure alternating magnetic fields, commonly used for assessing electromagnetic interference and exposure to electromagnetic fields. DC Gaussmeters, on the other hand, are specifically for measuring static or direct current magnetic fields, often used in applications involving permanent magnets or magnetized materials. Both types are portable and offer convenient and accurate measurements of magnetic fields in various industrial, scientific, and environmental settings.</p></p>
