<p><ul><li>Two-way Pressure</li><li>Unidirectional Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure microsensors are used in various applications to measure pressure variations. Two-way pressure microsensors can measure pressure changes in both positive and negative directions, providing a more comprehensive analysis of pressure fluctuations. On the other hand, unidirectional pressure microsensors can only measure pressure changes in one direction, limiting their applications to specific contexts. Both types of pressure microsensors have their unique benefits and are used in different industries based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
