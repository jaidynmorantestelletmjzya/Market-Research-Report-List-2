<p><ul><li>Fixed-Bed Type Gasifier</li><li>Fluidized-Bed Type Gasifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gasifier market consists of two main types: Fixed-Bed Type Gasifier and Fluidized-Bed Type Gasifier. Fixed-Bed Type Gasifiers use a stationary bed of fuel while the fluidized-bed type gasifiers use a bed of sand or other particulate material that is fluidized by an upward flow of air or other gas. Fixed-bed gasifiers are simpler in design and are suitable for small-scale applications, while fluidized-bed gasifiers offer better heat transfer and higher efficiency, making them ideal for larger-scale operations.</p></p>
