<p><p>Flexible Alternating Current Transmission Systems (FACTS) refer to a range of technologies used to enhance the controllability and power transfer capability of AC transmission systems. The FACTS market includes shunt compensation, which involves adding reactive power support at specific points in the grid, and series compensation, which involves inserting capacitors or inductors into the transmission line to enhance voltage regulation and power flow control. Both shunt and series compensation technologies play a critical role in improving the efficiency and reliability of power systems.</p></p>
