<p><p>The Automatical Magnetic Linear Encoder market includes two main types: Voltage Output Type and Open Collector Output Type. </p><p>Voltage Output Type encoders provide a direct voltage signal which varies with the position of the linear encoder. On the other hand, Open Collector Output Type encoders provide a digital signal output, where the output is either high or low depending on the position of the linear encoder.</p><p>Both types cater to different needs and applications within the industry, offering flexibility and accuracy in position sensing.</p></p>
