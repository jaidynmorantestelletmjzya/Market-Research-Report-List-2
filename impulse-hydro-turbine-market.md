<p><p>Pelton Hydro Turbine and Cross Flow Hydro Turbine are both types of impulse hydro turbines used in the hydropower industry. The Pelton Hydro Turbine is known for its high efficiency and is ideal for high head, low flow applications. On the other hand, the Cross Flow Hydro Turbine is suitable for low head, medium flow conditions. Both turbines harness the kinetic energy of water and convert it into mechanical energy to generate electricity, making them essential components of hydroelectric power plants.</p></p>
