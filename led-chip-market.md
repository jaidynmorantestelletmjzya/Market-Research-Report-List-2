<p><strong>The LED Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED chip market consists of various types including lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chips are the traditional type where the contacts are placed on the top of the chip. Vertical chips have contacts on the bottom, allowing for better heat dissipation. Flip chips are the latest technology, with the contacts facing downwards for improved thermal performance. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the lighting industry, offering distinct advantages in terms of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
