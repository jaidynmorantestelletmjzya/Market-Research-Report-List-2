<p><strong>The Vehicle Lead Acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Battery</li><li>Valve Regulated Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Lead Acid Battery market consists of two main types: Open Battery and Valve Regulated Battery. Open Battery, also known as flooded battery, requires regular maintenance and checks on electrolyte levels. It is commonly used in older vehicles and for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Valve Regulated Battery, or sealed battery, is maintenance-free and has a valve that regulates the release of gas. It is more suitable for modern vehicles and applications where minimal maintenance is preferred.</p></p>
