<p><p>A semi-automatic fluorescence microplate reader requires some manual input from the user during the reading process, such as setting parameters or loading plates. This type of reader is more cost-effective and is suitable for laboratories with lower throughput. On the other hand, a fully automatic fluorescence microplate reader operates without the need for manual intervention, offering higher throughput and efficiency. It is ideal for high-volume laboratories that require fast and accurate reading of large numbers of samples.</p></p>
