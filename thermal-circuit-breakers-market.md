<p><ul><li>Integral Type</li><li>PCB Mounting</li><li>Snap-in Mounting</li><li>Panel Mounting</li><li>Plug-in Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal circuit breakers are categorized into different types based on their mounting options and applications. Integral type breakers are built into a device or equipment for internal protection. PCB mounting breakers are designed to be directly mounted onto a PCB board for circuit protection. Snap-in mounting breakers can be easily snapped into place on a panel. Panel mounting breakers are installed onto the panel for easy access and operation. Plug-in type breakers can be plugged into a socket for quick installation and replacement.</p></p>
