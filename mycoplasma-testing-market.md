<p><p>Mycoplasma testing market includes various types of testing methods such as PCR, ELISA, Enzymatic Methods, and DNA Staining. PCR is a sensitive method that detects mycoplasma DNA. ELISA is an immunoassay technique that detects mycoplasma antigens. Enzymatic methods involve detecting enzyme activity produced by mycoplasma. DNA staining methods utilize dyes to visualize mycoplasma DNA under a microscope. Each testing method offers unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and ease of use for detecting mycoplasma contamination.</p></p>
