<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Special Purpose Logic IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design IC</li><li>Standard-cell Based IC</li><li>Gate-array Based IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Special Purpose Logic ICs are designed for specific applications and are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. Full-custom Design ICs are highly customized chips designed for a specific application, offering high performance and efficiency but requiring a longer development time and higher cost.</p><p>2. Standard-cell Based ICs are pre-designed blocks of logic cells that can be assembled in various configurations, offering a balance between performance, cost, and development time.</p><p>3. Gate-array Based ICs consist of a prefabricated grid of logic gates that can be interconnected to create custom designs quickly and cost-effectively, although with limited flexibility compared to full-custom designs.</p></p>
