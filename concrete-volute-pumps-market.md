<p><p>Concrete volute pumps are most commonly available in two types: single stage volute pumps and multistage volute pumps. Single stage volute pumps are designed to handle low to moderate flow rates, while multistage volute pumps are capable of handling higher flow rates by utilizing multiple impellers. Single stage pumps are typically used for smaller applications, while multistage pumps are used for larger scale projects such as water treatment plants or industrial processes. Both types offer efficient and reliable pumping solutions for various industries.</p></p>
