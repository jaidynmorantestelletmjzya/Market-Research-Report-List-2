<p><p>Dual-band front-end modules market consists of different types of components like power amplifiers (PA), low noise amplifiers (LNA), switches, and others. Power amplifiers (PA) are responsible for boosting the signal strength, while low noise amplifiers (LNA) reduce the noise in the signal. Switches help in controlling the signal flow between different components. Other components in the market include filters, duplexers, and matching networks that help in maintaining signal integrity and improving overall performance of the front-end modules.</p></p>
