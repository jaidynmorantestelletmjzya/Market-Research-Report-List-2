<p><p>Gas-insulated Transmission Lines (GIL) are used for high voltage power transmission over long distances. They come in various types based on their installation methods. Aboveground Installation involves mounting the lines on towers, Tunnel Installation is used for transmission lines passing through underground tunnels, Vertical Installation is for lines installed on tall structures like buildings, and Direct Burial involves burying the lines underground. Each type of installation is chosen based on factors such as terrain, cost, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
