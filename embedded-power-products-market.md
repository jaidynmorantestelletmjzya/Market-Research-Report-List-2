<p><p>Embedded power products refer to devices that are used to provide power to embedded systems in various applications. The market for these products can be categorized into AC-DC Power Supplies, DC/DC Converters, and Others. AC-DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) from a power source to direct current (DC) for electronic devices. DC/DC converters regulate and convert DC voltage levels for efficient power distribution. The "Others" category includes power management ICs, voltage regulators, and power modules utilized in embedded systems.</p></p>
