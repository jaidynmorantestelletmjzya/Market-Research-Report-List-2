<p><p>DC power connectors are available in three main types based on their mounting style: surface mount, panel mount, and through hole. Surface mount connectors are mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, while panel mount connectors are designed to be mounted onto a panel or enclosure. Through hole connectors are mounted by inserting their leads through holes on a circuit board and soldering them in place. Each type of connector offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
