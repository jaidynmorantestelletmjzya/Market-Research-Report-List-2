<p><p>Oven Controlled Quartz Crystal Oscillators (OCXOs) are available in two main market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount OCXOs are soldered directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them compact and ideal for high-density applications. Through Hole OCXOs require holes to be drilled in the circuit board for mounting, offering a more secure and reliable connection. Both types provide precise and stable frequency control through temperature regulation, making them suitable for demanding applications in industries like telecommunications, aerospace, and defense.</p></p>
